I have a love for...

Stories

Fantasy stories in particular. I can't get enough of them, and it doesn't matter what form they come in - movies, games or books. When I was younger, I remember the moment when I realised that I had run out of fantasy books to read. I had read all of the major ones, from Wheel of Times to Game of Thrones, from Garth Nix to Patrick Rothfuss.

Boxing

I love the sport of boxing, and I strongly believe that boxing training is greatly beneficial for not just your physical health (that goes without saying) but also your mental and spiritual well-being. There's good reason why so many writers and philosophers have been so taken with boxing.

People

Aleksandr Solzhenitsyn once wrote "The line between good and evil runs through every human heart". I try to live my life following those words. When I meet people I assume that they have something valuable to add and an important story to tell. I try to listen closely to what people have to say and I treat everyone with the same respect. Too sentimental for an About Me page? Probably, but I don't think I'm capable of writing an About Me section without being a little sentimental.
